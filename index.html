The Forging of GMM Token

(an excerpt from the Chronicles of Micko)

In an age long past, when the chain was still young, one known as Micko set forth to craft a token of his own. By will alone, the idea was forged into form. Thus, GMM Token was born.

Whispers of its creation traveled across distant realms spoken by wanderers, scribes, and adventurers of the digital dominions. Some told of a forging flame, others of the unseen will that shaped its essence...
